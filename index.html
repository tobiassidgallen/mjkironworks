About Us

At MJK Iron Works, we bring over a decade of hands-on experience in custom metal fabrication. From elegant gates to durable stair railings, our craftsmanship reflects precision, artistry, and pride. We serve homeowners, builders, and interior designers who value lasting quality and refined detail.
